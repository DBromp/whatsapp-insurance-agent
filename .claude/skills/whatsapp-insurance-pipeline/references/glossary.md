# PT-BR insurance glossary

Brazilian Portuguese auto-insurance terms that appear in `message_body` and need consistent interpretation.

| PT-BR term | English | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| apólice | policy | The issued insurance contract |
| cotação | quote | Pre-sale price estimate |
| seguro auto | auto insurance | Product category |
| seguradora | insurer | Provider company |
| franquia | deductible | Lead-paid portion on claim |
| cobertura | coverage | What's included in the policy |
| sinistro | claim / incident | Accident or theft event |
| segurado | insured | The lead after closing |
| corretor | broker | Sales agent (sometimes used for `agent`) |
| vistoria | inspection | Pre-policy vehicle inspection |
| renovação | renewal | Annual policy renewal |
| prêmio | premium | Annual policy price |
| indenização | indemnity / payout | Settlement amount |
| placa | license plate | Identifier on the car |
| chassi | chassis / VIN | Vehicle ID number |
| RENAVAM | RENAVAM | National vehicle registry ID |
| acionar o seguro | file a claim | Trigger insurance |
| terceiros | third-party | Coverage for damage to others |
| casco | comprehensive (own vehicle) | Coverage for one's own car |
| RCF-V | optional liability cover | Responsabilidade Civil Facultativa Veículos |
| APP | personal accident cover | Acidentes Pessoais de Passageiros |
| assistência 24h | 24/7 roadside assistance | Standard add-on |

## Competitor brands to watch for

Porto Seguro, Bradesco Seguros, Azul Seguros, SulAmérica, Liberty Seguros, Allianz, Tokio Marine, Mapfre, HDI Seguros, Itaú Seguros, Sompo

## Vehicle vocabulary tells

- "tô" / "to" / "tava" — informal "I am/was"
- "boleto" — bank slip (Brazilian payment method, often comes up in negotiation)
- "parcelar" — pay in installments
- "à vista" — pay in full
- "desconto" — discount
- "barato" / "caro" — cheap / expensive (price objections)
